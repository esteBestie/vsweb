Apple Display XDR

The most advanced display ever created.

The Apple Display XDR is the most advanced display ever created. It features a stunning 32-inch Retina 6K display with a resolution of 6016 x 3384. The display also supports HDR10 and Dolby Vision, so you can enjoy the most realistic and immersive viewing experience possible.

  • 32-inch Retina 6K display
  • 6016 x 3384 resolution
  • HDR10 and Dolby Vision support
  • 10-bit color depth
  • 1,000,000:1 contrast ratio
  • 500 nits of brightness
  • 1600 nits of peak brightness
